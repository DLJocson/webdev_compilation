My Daily Routine

Morning Routine (Ordered List)

  1. Wake up and check phone for updates (emails, messages, and my reading lists).
  2. Take 20-30 minutes to read a chapter or two of a story that I am currently reading.
  3. Once up, I always drink at least 500ml of water before having breakfast, which are usually bread and eggs.
  4. After I'm done eating, I wash dishes and help out in other chores where needed.
  5. Then, if I need to go out later, I will check my bag and make sure that I have all the necessary items.
  6. Once that's done, I take a quick shower to freshen up, usually just a quick rinse and brush my teeth.
  7. Afterwards, I open my laptop and create a quick to do list for the day.
